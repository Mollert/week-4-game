$$ Play the Gem Game $$

Click on the start button to begin a new game.

A new value for you to match will appear.

Each gem has a value and as you click on them the value will be added to your running total.

To win, you will have to equal the match value. Wins and losses are tallied.

To try a new match number, click the reset button for another chance.
